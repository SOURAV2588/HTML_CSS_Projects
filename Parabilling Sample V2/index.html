Paragliding

Team Parabilling is a group of hawks of paragliding world who are famously known as ''Team Flow''. All the pilots are aces in their own genre with experience of flying in different places in India for past 10 years. All of them have proved their mettle in both national and international paragliding competitions and now they are all dedicated towards providing you the most supernatural experience of your life, so come and let us give you the most extravagant experience of your life.

Trekking

Bir(Billing) is situated at the edge Dhauladhar mountains where snow-capped peaks hover like static clouds above forests of Spruce, Deodar and Rhododendron making it a perfect spot to explore the flora and fauna of himalayas and test limits of your own might while venturing in these majestic mountain ranges .So Parabilling gives you an opportunity to rediscover your own conscious self by getting absorbed in the true spirited essence of trekking in these mountains with us. We invite you to come and enjoy these magical moments with your family or friends and discover the effortless familiarity that defies the limits of mere genetical, societal and emotional ties in your life.

Camping

Camping is living in cool solitude of mountains and trees where chants of gusting winds and chirping of birds spins a music which puts our weary minds at ease and we discover new rhythms in our soul . Team Parabilling invites you to enjoy some time living under the pitch black sky filled with bright stars in middle of wilderness where there is fire and friendship to keep your body and soul worm, food and laughter to fill your tummy and some natural music for your ghost stories. Come and give us opportunity to serve you in one the most spectacular moments in your life
